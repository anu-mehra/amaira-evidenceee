Valla Bypass Redevelopment Project — Amritsar, Punjab

In my investigation of the congested bypass leading to my school in Amritsar, I conducted a comprehensive field study—collecting aerial and on-site photography, mapping traffic flow patterns, interviewing local residents and business owners, and documenting their lived experiences via video. I analyzed the root causes of the bottleneck in a detailed research report, then designed a 3D intervention model that reorganizes traffic lanes, introduces a dedicated service road for vendors and school access, and redefines pedestrian zones to enhance safety and movement. By merging community voices, empirical data and spatial design, the project illustrates how architecture and urban planning can restore order and resilience in everyday urban life.
